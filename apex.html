Apex Legends: Meet the Legends

Alter - The Phasing Fighter

Alter is a female legend who can phase through walls and obstacles, making her a tricky opponent to pin down. With her unique abilities, she can create rifts in the battlefield, allowing her and her team to reposition quickly and escape dangerous situations.

Abilities:

  • Tactical: Phase Rift - Create a temporary rift that allows Alter to phase through objects for a short duration, providing a tactical advantage in combat.
  • Passive: Phasing Agility - Alter gains increased movement speed while phasing, making her harder to hit.
    • Ultimate: Teleportation Device - Deploy a device that creates a teleportation point, allowing Alter and her teammates to teleport away from a fight quickly.

Crypto - The Surveillance Expert

Crypto is a stealthy, recon-focused legend who excels at gathering information and disabling enemy defenses. Using his surveillance drone, Crypto monitors the battlefield from a distance, giving his squad the edge in combat.

Abilities:

  • Tactical: Surveillance Drone - Deploy a drone that scans for enemies and can hack into enemy systems.
  • Passive: Neurolink - Enemies detected by the drone within 30 meters of Crypto are automatically revealed to his team.
  • Ultimate: Drone EMP - Detonate an EMP blast from your drone, dealing shield damage and disabling traps.
